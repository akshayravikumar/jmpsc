Eligibility:

To be eligble for prizes, you must be under the age of 14. Keep in mind that this test is geared
towards middle school students. If you are older, you may participate, but you will not recieve a prize
should you win.

Date:

The contest date is July 10th. Please take a look at the FAQ section if you can not make it to the test.

Sprint Round:

Contestants are given 40 minutes to solve 20 questions, each question is worth 3 points, for a total of 60 points.

Accuracy Round:

Contestants are given 45 minutes to solve 15 questions, each question is worth 4 points, for a total of 60 points.

Invitational Round:

The combined score of the sprint and accuracy rounds determine whether or not the contestant is qualified
for the invitaitonal round. Contestants are given 60 minutes to solve 20 questions, each question is worth 15
points, for a total of 300 points.

Tiebreaker:

Contestants are given 15 minutes maximum to solve 1 question, score is determined by this formula:
50 - (number of seconds taken/20). This means that the faster the correct answer is submitted, more
points will be recieved.
