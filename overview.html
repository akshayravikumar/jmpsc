Eligibility:

To be eligble for prizes, you must be between ages 11-14. If you are younger, you will be eligible for prizes. Keep in mind that this test is geared towards middle school students. If you are older, you may participate, but you will not recieve a prize should you win.

Date:

The test will be held on Saturday, June 19th, 3 pm pst, or 6 pm est. Please take a look at the FAQ section if you can not make it to the test.

Sprint Round:

Contestants are given 40 minutes to solve 20 short answer questions. Each question has one answer.

Accuracy Round:

Contestants are given 45 minutes to solve 15 short answer questions. Each question has one answer.

Invitational Round:

Contestants are given 60 minutes to solve 6 short answer questions. Each question has one answer.
